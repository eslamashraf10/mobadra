Welcome to Initiative Management System

Report issues, track initiatives, and collaborate across departments seamlessly. Empower your organization with a centralized platform that enhances communication, improves transparency, and speeds up problem resolution — all in one place.

Report an Issue

Reporting an Issue

A simple and intuitive form that allows employees to submit issues with full details, attachments, and priority levels. Each submission is automatically assigned a unique tracking number, enabling quick follow-up and transparent communication between departments.

Case Management

Each reported issue is routed to the appropriate department automatically. Supervisors can assign responsible team members, monitor progress in real time, and receive automated notifications until the case is resolved completely.

Facilitation & Follow-up

Encourages collaboration by allowing departments to suggest solutions, schedule meetings, and document decisions. Every action is recorded in a clear timeline, making it easy to track the full history of the issue and evaluate response efficiency.
